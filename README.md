#  Business Case Study  E-commerce Growth Strategy

##  Problem Statement

The e-commerce business aims to improve revenue growth and customer retention. Current data indicates unstable revenue trends and a low number of repeat customers.

 **Core Question:**
How can revenue be increased sustainably?

---

##  Objectives

* Identify key revenue drivers
* Analyze customer behavior
* Improve customer retention
* Define a scalable business growth strategy

##  Key Insights

* Overall revenue is very low → limited business scale
* Customer base is extremely small
* Repeat purchase rate is low (~40%) → weak retention
* Revenue trend is inconsistent
* High dependency on a few customers
* Significant churn risk among inactive users

---

##  Business Strategy

### 1️ Customer Acquisition

* Launch referral programs
* Optimize paid marketing campaigns
* Leverage social media channels

---

### 2️ Retention Strategy

* Introduce loyalty programs (points, cashback)
* Run personalized email campaigns
* Use push notifications for re-engagement

---

### 3️ Increase AOV (Revenue Growth)

* Introduce bundle offers
* Set free shipping thresholds
* Implement cross-sell and upsell strategies

---

### 4️ High-Value Customer Focus

* Offer VIP benefits and early access
* Launch premium memberships
* Provide exclusive discounts

---

### 5️ Churn Reduction

* Run win-back campaigns
* Offer discounts to inactive users
* Send reminder and reactivation emails

---

##  Final Conclusion

The business is currently limited by a small customer base and weak retention. Sustainable revenue growth requires a balanced focus on acquisition, retention, and monetization.

 **Sustainable Growth = Acquisition + Retention + Monetization**
