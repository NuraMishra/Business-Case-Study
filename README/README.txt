Problem Statement
The goal of this project is to analyze an e-commerce dataset to identify key factors affecting revenue and provide actionable strategies to improve overall business performance.

The analysis focuses on:
Customer behavior
Revenue trends
Purchase patterns
Customer retention

Key Insights
Total revenue generated is ₹4000 from 5 customers, indicating a very small customer base and limited scale of operations.
The average order value is approximately ₹571, showing moderate spending behavior but room for improvement.
Only 40% of customers are repeat buyers, which highlights weak customer retention and a high dependency on one-time purchases.
Revenue shows significant monthly fluctuations, with a peak in February (₹1700) and a sharp drop in April (₹400), indicating unstable demand.
A small number of customers (Customer 1 and 3) contribute a large share of total revenue, suggesting dependency on high-value users.
Some customers show high recency values (e.g., 112 days), indicating inactivity and potential churn risk.


Business Recommendations
1. Improve Customer Retention
Introduce loyalty programs (points, cashback, rewards)
Use personalized email and notification campaigns to re-engage inactive users

2. Increase Average Order Value
Offer bundle deals and product combinations
Set free delivery thresholds (e.g., above ₹999) to encourage higher spending

3. Target High-Value Customers
Provide exclusive discounts and early access offers
Create premium membership programs for top customers

4. Re-engage Inactive Customers
Launch “win-back” campaigns for customers with high recency
Provide special discount coupons to bring them back.

5. Improve Customer Acquisition
Launch referral programs
Optimize digital marketing campaigns to increase customer base

Conclusion
The analysis reveals that revenue is currently driven by a small set of high-value customers, while overall customer retention remains low. Additionally, revenue trends are inconsistent across months.
To achieve sustainable growth, the business should focus on improving customer retention, re-engaging inactive users, and increasing average order value rather than relying solely on acquiring new customers.
